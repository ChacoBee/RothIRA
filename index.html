                Portfolio Health Assessment

                Portfolio Health Score (PHS) = (RRB x 30%) + (DIV x 25%) + (DR x 20%) + (LQ x 10%) + (GA x 15%).

                PHS uses a 0-10 scale. Higher scores indicate a balanced, well diversified portfolio aligned with long-term hangar objectives.

                Portfolio Health Score Breakdown

                Component Weight Score (0-10) Live context
                Risk-Return Balance (RRB) 30% - Focus on Sharpe ratio, volatility and expected return.
                Diversification & Correlation (DIV) 25% - Mix of asset classes, regions and correlation structure.
                Drawdown Resilience (DR) 20% - Considers beta, realised volatility and drawdown history.
                Liquidity & Simplicity (LQ) 10% - Looks at ETF coverage versus concentrated or illiquid positions.
                Goal Alignment (GA) 15% - Measures how the mix supports long-horizon hangar objectives.
                Portfolio Health Score 100% - Weighted blend of the five core pillars.
